How to burn calories while you are sleeping

Lose weight in a dream - a fairytale for lazy, is not it? Nevertheless, at night, a body continues to spend energy, and it can be done so that it takes it from fat!
Sleeping time is a great time for losing weight. Think: you do not eat at least 8 hours while you are asleep. In this case, a body still metabolizes, which spends calories on the work of the heart, breathing, maintaining body temperature, hair and nails growth, the synthesis of hormones. But will fat be split or will energy come from other sources? Everything depends on you!

What should you do?
Do not eat at night fast carbohydrates (sugar, flour, bread, baking) after 5-6 pm. Otherwise, a body will have enough glucose, which a body will use instead of fat. Replace flour with cereals, bran and vegetables - high fiber content facilitates the utilization of fat.
Have supper at 7-8 pm. If you go to bed late and need a small snack, try to eat no more than 200 kcal.
Include in the evening menu low-fat dairy products - kefir, yogurt, milk, fermented baked milk. All of them help to fall asleep, because they contain amino acid tryptophan, which reduces the level of stress.
Do not eat foods that are high in protein - meat, eggs, cottage cheese, cheese. With them, you will surely get an excess of tyrosine, which excites the nervous system and awakens the appetite.
After 5-6pm, avoid carbonated drinks, tomato sauce, coffee and citrus. They are best used in the morning, as they interfere with the night burning of fat.
How to avoid insomnia
Turn off the light! Not only during sleep, but also about half an hour before it. The fact is that even 5 minutes of bright light - including from the TV screen - significantly lower the level of the hormone melatonin. This hormone is responsible for a sound healthy sleep. So make a computer screen not so bright and turn on the night light instead of the top light.
Do you do fitness? Active training should end at least 2 hours before bedtime. Heavy loads excite a body too much and prevent you from falling asleep quickly and qualitatively relaxing. But if intensive training takes place before eight in the evening, it, on the contrary, will improve sleep.
Try to go to bed between 22.45 and 23.15. During this period most of us have draw straws. But, it is worth overpowering yourself to watch or finish reading something interesting, a body can increase the stress hormone cortisol, which reduces drowsiness. Opening a second wind will help you stay without sleep for up to 2am. But how to sleep necessary 6.5-8 hours in this case?
Lack of sleep makes you eat more. Scientists from Great Britain came to this conclusion. They found the relationship between the duration of sleep and the consumption of calories the next day. The researchers conducted a series of eleven studies on 172 tests people. According to the results, an insufficient sleep - less than seven hours - leads to the fact that the next day a person consumes about 385 calories more.
Experts say that the whole thing is in the hormone that is called leptin. This hormone is responsible for suppressing appetite, due to it brain receives a signal that saturation has occurred and it is necessary to stop consuming food. In case of lack of sleep, the level of leptin in a body decreases, so those for whom an eight-hour sleep is a luxury have hunger more often. In addition, they often show a tendency to harmful foods, because with their help it is easier to satisfy imaginary hunger.
