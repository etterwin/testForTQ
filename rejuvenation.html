Rejuvenation

When should we start coping with aging?

The earlier a person begins to resist aging, the better.
Old age comes up unnoticed. It does not have a definite beginning. Although there are critical points. It is considered, for example, that it begins when menopause occurs. From the point of view of evolution, an organism that has fulfilled its reproductive function is no longer needed for nature and therefore is rejected. The first signs: a person is more tired, worse endures the load, loses adaptive abilities, his efficiency decreases. But all this is very individual ...

Many people age prematurely due to unfavorable environmental conditions and an indifferent attitude towards themselves. In an era of economic crises, many, including young ones, are beginning to grow old much earlier than genetics. This is a reaction to psychological stress. Today in Russia you can often meet 25-, 30-, 35-year-old "old men" - a passport says that they are still quite young, but according to biological age, it is prematurely decrepit. There are a lot of reasons for this: non-demanded in the labor market, abuse of alcohol and drugs, a disorderly lifestyle and, as a consequence, depression.
It is known that a high level of education adds years: a person who graduated only primary or secondary school, lives an average of 6 years less than those who graduated from a university or college. Therefore, it is necessary to invest money not only in geriatrics, although it is the duty of society in relation to previous generations, but also in support of relatively young people, to instill in them the skills of a healthy lifestyle, to raise their level of education. That means to begin to fight with aging in youth. And then to prolong not "life in general", but the person's ability to work and his interest to life.
As for prevention of premature aging, it must start at the age of 30. But in general, the sooner, the better. And it should be remembered that living up to 500 years is unreal, no matter how hard we try. This is a pipe dream. And medical science does not help here. The main thing is not how many years to live, but how.
Each of us and each generation has a biological age. Sometimes it is significantly different from the calendar.
The main thing in the extension of personal youth is the way of life, moral spirit and the will of a person. There are many things affecting it: daily rhythm, diet, the presence of carcinogens in food, income level, habitat, setting for a healthy life, and even if the light burns in your bedroom at night. For example, optimism is as a quality of a character changes the body's key settings: resistance to disease, adaptation to environmental changes, changes in the psychological and economic climate. Many elderly people live actively exclusively on willpower, and this helps them to keep their body and spirit cheerful at any age.
Have you ever wondered why some people put up with the situation, while others continue to struggle for the quality of their lives? What is it - free choice or fate? Everyone decides for himself: to live with dignity and for long, or to drink vodka and die before they reach 45 ... All this is not a joke, but, often, a bargain with the devil. For example, it is known that people who feel younger than their real age, reduced the risk of death: the inner sense of youth and optimism create a protective effect that does not give a person to die early.
